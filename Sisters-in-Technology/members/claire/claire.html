Bio

    Claire Jensen is a student at South High School in the class of 2020. She has been a member of Sisters-n-Technology since 2017, and she claims to be way too busy to come to most of the meetings. Claire enjoys coding in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. Next on her list to learn is C. She has had two summer internships, one at Toole Design Group, and one at Amazon. In the rare case that she has free time, Claire likes to play soccer, snowboard, code, read, play video games, take pictures, and build circuits. You can find her fire Spotify playlists below. Claire hopes to go to college, major in computer science, and either take a double major, or a minor. Her top choices in terms of schools are; Bowdoin, Tufts, Harvey Mudd, University of Chicago, Amherst, and Carleton. Claire hopes to create her own business and become an entrepreneur someday.
